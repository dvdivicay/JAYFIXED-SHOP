The Ultimate Guide to Fixed Gear Bikes

Discover everything you need to know about fixed gear bikes, from their history to maintenance tips. Fixed gear bikes, also known as fixies, are bicycles that have a drivetrain with no freewheel mechanism. This means that the pedals are always in motion when the bike is moving. Fixies are popular for their simplicity, low maintenance, and the unique riding experience they offer. In this guide, we'll cover the history of fixed gear bikes, their benefits, and some essential maintenance tips to keep your fixie in top condition.

How to Customize Your Fixie

Learn how to personalize your fixed gear bike with custom parts and accessories. One of the great things about fixies is the ability to customize them to suit your style and preferences. From colorful chains and handlebars to unique saddles and pedals, the possibilities are endless. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of customizing your fixie, including tips on choosing the right parts and accessories, and how to install them. Whether you want to make your bike stand out or improve its performance, we've got you covered.

  • Upgrade the handlebars for better control and comfort.
  • Install a new saddle for improved comfort on long rides.
  • Replace the pedals with ones that offer better grip.
  • Add colorful chains to give your bike a unique look.
  • Install new tires for better traction and performance.
  • Upgrade the wheels for a smoother ride.
  • Add custom decals to personalize your bike.
  • Install a new crankset for better power transfer.
  • Replace the brake system for improved safety.
  • Add lights and reflectors for better visibility at night.
